Purpose of a header

Introductory content for its nearest ancestor sectioning content or sectioning root element. A header typically contains a group of introductory or navigational aids. When the nearest ancestor sectioning content or sectioning root element is the body element, then it applies to the whole page.

Artcle content

The article element specifies independent, self-contained content.

Purpose of the section element.

According to W3C's HTML5 documentation: A section is a thematic grouping of content, typically with a heading. A home page could normally be split into sections for introduction, content, and contact information.

Purpose of the footer element

.

The footer element specifies a footer for a document or section. A footer element should contain information about its containing element. A footer typically contains the author of the document, copyright information, links to terms of use, contact information, etc.
